    Cycling

    A low-impact way to boost you cardiovascular health (that’s your heart and blood vessels!). This exercise type also builds strength, without putting too much pressure on your knees.

    Powerlifting

    A powerful way to boost your overall strength and support healthy joints (including your knees and hips!). This exercise style also improves muscle mass and bone health, without the high-impact stress of activities like running.

    Yoga

    A gentle way to enhance your flexibility and balance (great for your muscles and joints!). This exercise type also helps build strength and reduce stress, without putting too much strain on your body."
